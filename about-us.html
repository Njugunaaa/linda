Our story is shaping tomorrow's innovators

Linda Future Initiative is a youth-led organization in Thika East, Kenya, empowering communities through civic engagement, wellness advocacy, and environmental restoration. We mobilize young people to drive change in health, peacebuilding, and climate-smart livelihoods while supporting vulnerable students with education and mentorship to create a resilient, self-reliant future.

Our core vision

To create a future where mental health is prioritized and accessible to every one in rural kenya especially the youth, fostering a community that thrives on holistic well-being and resilience.

Our main mission

To raise mental health awarenesss in rural areas of central kenya through physical activities, community forums, and educational sessions, working closely with the local community, organizations, government bodies and well wishers.
